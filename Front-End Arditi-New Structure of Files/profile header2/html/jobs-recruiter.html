Job description
We are hiring driven Warehouse Associates! ·Currently enrolled as a university student
·Basic knowledge of English or German language
·No prior work experience is required
·Physical endurance (Up to 8 hours standing/walking; Max: 20kg lifting)
·Able to work independently and within a team
